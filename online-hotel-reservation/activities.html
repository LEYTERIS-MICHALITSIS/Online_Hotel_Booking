Activities
Golf
We provide professional golf tuition to players of every skill level.Friends of the game will enjoy the landscape and experience a masterful course design.The practice facilities are also available to help develop your game.
Tennis
Our facilities consist of 2 clay courts and 2 grass courts.Our professional tennis coach can improve your game with structured lessons that have a 1-hour duration ,and are focused on improving your technique(reservations required).All the equipment you need, from tennis balls to raquets are provided by the reception.
Scuba Diving
If you want to try scuba diving then you are at the right place.Our professional coaches can lead you through your journey and provide you the necessary eqipment.You will learn how to use your scuba diving equipment and most of all you will have fun exploring the underwater world.
